Dancing Sound

Description

Taking on the role of a synesthete, this abstract animation links sounds to visuals giving a glimpse into what one may experience by have such abilities. The type of instrument and pitch at which the note is played in the music determines the shape and color of the visuals that are displayed. Drawing from color psychology, different beats and rhythms in the musical composition directly connected to varying levels of hue, value, and saturation of the colors. Simple shapes and colors are used in alignment with the art of abstraction.

Software: Adobe After Effects
Audio: Apologize by OneRepublic


Research Question: How are color and sound connected?

Timeline: 3 Weeks

Constraints: Involves color
