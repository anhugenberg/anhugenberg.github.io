Yellow Submarine VR Game

Description

Yellow submarine is a virtual reality immersive experience in which a player swims in an underwater environment with the goal of identifying eight different types of fish, 2 of which are nearly extinct. Aside from this goal, players are encouraged to explore the underwater world and enjoy the warmth, tranquility and calmness it brings. The purpose of this project is to provide an opportunity for people who can not snorkel or dive in the ocean, for various reasons such as disabilities, restricted location, and or mental blocks/fear, to experience this unique activity via virtual reality. Also, by integrating nearly extinct species, our project encourages players to look more closely at how they as individuals may be impacting the health and wellbeing of the oceans and in particular of other coexisting living organisms. Overall, it educates players and expands on previous understandings about just how diverse, complex and beautiful life on Earth is.

Software: Unity & Blender

Problem Statement: Virtual under water experiences for people who can not snorkel or dive in the ocean are limited.

Timeline: 3 Months

Constraints: Built in Unity, original models, impact social good
