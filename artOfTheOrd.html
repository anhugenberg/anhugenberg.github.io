Art of the Ordinary

Description

An agamograph art installation that recontextualizes and redefines our relationship to mundane, everyday objects through pattern, texture, color, and contrast to make visible what otherwise would be overlooked. By artistically abstracting ordinary objects, new ideas and meanings are formed.

Medium: Installation/Sculpture Art

Partner: Lily Pelton

Problem Statement: General lack of awareness and appreciation for everyday objects in our lives that inconspicuously affect our overall well being and happiness.

Timeline: 6 Months

Role: Co-led all phases from research to ideation to building and testing

Constraints: Involves creativity, technology, design and interaction
