Treasure Palace

Description

Treasure Palace is a gaming system in which the user interacts, takes action, and responds to a Processing sketch that visually displays the game interface on a computer. The theme of the game follows a pirate who scavenges for treasure on an island in the middle of the sea with the ultimate goal of finding as many diamonds, rubies and emeralds as possible after 10 rolls of the dice. The purpose of the game is to allow people of all ages to relax in times of stress and increase creativity, problem-solving skills and emotional wellbeing.

Software: Processing
Hardware: Arduino
