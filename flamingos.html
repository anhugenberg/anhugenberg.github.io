Mysterious Creatures

Description

Digitally hand drawn using Adobe Fresco, this animation focuses on the theme of “turning the tables” in which humans are the attractions at the zoo for other animals to visit. More specifically, it follows the story of a young flamingo and his mother going to the zoo to see the newborn babies. Rotoscoping and doubling were the main techniques used to create the thousands of frames used in the final animation.

Software: Adobe Fresco & Adobe After Effects
Audio: No. 9 Esther’s Waltz by Esther Abrami


Goal: Create an animation that conveys the theme of "turning the tables".

Timeline: 3 Weeks

Constraints: Use of rotoscoping and doubling.
